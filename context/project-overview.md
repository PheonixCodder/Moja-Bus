# Project Overview

## About Moja Ride

**Moja Ride is the digital transportation infrastructure for intercity bus operators in Côte d'Ivoire.**

This is not just a bus ticket booking app. We are building a **two-sided marketplace platform** that connects passengers with bus companies while providing bus operators with a **complete business management system**.

Think of it as a combination of **Booking.com** (marketplace discovery), **Uber's booking experience** (seamless user flow), and a **complete ERP system** (business operations management) for bus operators.

---

## The Problem

### Current Situation
Today, intercity bus travel in Côte d'Ivoire is largely an **offline, manual, and fragmented** process.

### Problems for Passengers

#### 1. No Visibility
Passengers have **zero visibility** into available options before physically visiting stations:
- Which companies operate their desired route
- Which buses still have seats available
- Departure times
- Ticket prices
- Bus quality and amenities
- Travel duration

#### 2. Wasted Time
The search process is **extremely inefficient**:
```
Go to Station A → No tickets available
↓
Go to Station B → Only VIP tickets remain
↓
Go to Station C → Bus leaves tomorrow
↓
Finally find an available ticket
```
A process that should take **5 minutes** can take **half a day**.

#### 3. Unnecessary Transportation Costs
Passengers pay for taxis/local transport just to move between stations.

#### 4. No Easy Comparison
No central place to compare prices, departure times, operators, amenities, or seat availability.

#### 5. Last-Minute Uncertainty
Passengers may travel to a station only to discover the bus is full, delayed, or canceled.

### Problems for Bus Companies

#### 1. Manual Ticket Management
Rely on paper tickets, Excel sheets, phone calls, WhatsApp bookings, and handwritten records.

#### 2. Double Booking
Same seat may be sold twice without a centralized system.

#### 3. Difficult Fleet Management
Struggle to track buses, drivers, routes, daily trips, occupancy, and revenue.

#### 4. No Online Presence
Cannot be found online; customers must already know they exist.

#### 5. Lost Revenue
Customers give up after unsuccessful searches; sales are permanently lost.

#### 6. Poor Business Analytics
Cannot easily answer: which routes are most profitable? which buses are underutilized? what are monthly revenues?

## The Solution

Moja Ride is a **complete digital ecosystem for intercity bus transportation** in Côte d'Ivoire.

### Platform Components
| Component | Purpose | Target Users |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Passenger Mobile App | Search, book, manage tickets on-the-go | Travelers |
| Passenger Website | Search, book, manage tickets from desktop | Travelers |
| Operator Portal | Complete business management system | Bus Company Owners/Managers |
| Admin Dashboard | Platform oversight and management | Moja Ride Administrators |

### Passenger Experience
- Search trips: From → To → Date
- Compare all operators on route instantly
- Interactive seat map selection
- Digital tickets with QR codes
- Offline ticket access
- Real-time notifications (confirmations, reminders, delays)

### Operator Experience (Bus Company Portal)
- **Company Registration**: Create profile, upload documents, verification
- **Fleet Management**: Add/manage all buses with seat layouts
- **Route Management**: Define routes with stops, distances, durations
- **Schedule Management**: Create recurring or one-time trips
- **Seat Management**: Real-time seat tracking, prevent double booking
- **Booking Management**: Accept, cancel, modify, check-in, print tickets, issue refunds
- **Ticket & Pricing Management**: Base fares, discounts, promotions, VIP pricing
- **Revenue Dashboard**: Daily/weekly/monthly revenue, route performance, occupancy rates
- **Staff Management**: Assign roles and permissions

### Admin Dashboard
- Verify bus companies
- Manage users
- Resolve disputes
- Platform analytics
- Monitor transactions
- Content moderation
- System configuration
- Report generation

## Core User Flows

### Passenger Flow
1. **Search** - Enter origin, destination, date
2. **Compare** - See all operators, prices, times, ratings
3. **Select** - Choose trip, view seat map, select seats
4. **Book** - Enter details, choose payment, complete
5. **Receive** - Get digital ticket instantly
6. **Travel** - Show QR code, receive updates

### Operator Flow
1. **Register** - Create company profile, upload documents
2. **Setup** - Add fleet, define routes, create schedules
3. **Manage** - Monitor bookings, track inventory
4. **Operate** - Check in passengers, manage departures
5. **Analyze** - View revenue reports, track performance
